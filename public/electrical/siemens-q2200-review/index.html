The Siemens Q2200 is the most consistently reliable 200A main breaker for industrial panel upgrades in 2026. Who Should Buy This

✓ Buy the Siemens Q2200 if:

  • Your facility has high humidity — food processing, coastal, car washes, breweries

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the Siemens Q2200 in a Square D panel? +
No. Siemens strongly advises against using Q2200 breakers in non-Siemens panels, and doing so voids the panel warranty. The Q2200 is designed for Siemens EQ, Ultimate, ITE, and Gould load centers. If you have a Square D panel, specify the Square D QO2200 instead.
What is the difference between Q2200 and Q2200H? +
The Q2200 has a 10,000 AIC interrupting rating, suitable for most residential and light commercial services. The Q2200H (and Q2200BH) have a 22,000 AIC rating for high-fault commercial and industrial applications where the utility service may deliver higher fault currents. Always check your service entrance fault current rating before specifying.
How much does a Siemens Q2200 cost on Zoro vs Amazon? +
Pricing varies by quantity and supplier. On Zoro.com, the Q2200 typically runs $80–$100 for single units with contractor pricing available for volume orders. Amazon pricing is similar at $75–$110. For large commercial projects, purchasing through a local electrical distributor (Graybar, Rexel, Wesco) often yields better pricing with local support.
