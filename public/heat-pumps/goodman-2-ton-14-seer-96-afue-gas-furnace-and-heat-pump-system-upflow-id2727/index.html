This 2 Ton unit pairs an 80,000 BTU gas furnace with a matching 24,000 BTU heat pump, allowing contractors to install one integrated system rather than two separate units. Performance specs tell a compelling story. Two-stage burners modulate output based on demand, reducing energy waste during mild heating days. The variable-speed ECM blower adjusts to 1200 CFM maximum, matching airflow to actual load requirements rather than running full speed constantly. Frequently Asked Questions

Why does this 2 Ton system list 3 Ton cooling capacity in the specs? +
The condenser unit itself measures 3 Ton, but the matched furnace coil is 2 Ton, creating an intentional mismatch for single-stage operation. This design reduces short cycling and improves seasonal efficiency, though it sacrifices peak cooling output. Verify your load calculation supports this before ordering.
Can I install this in a tight basement or attic space? +
The unit measures 34.5 inches tall by 17.5 inches wide by 28.75 inches deep. It needs 18 inches of clearance above for return air and 3 feet of working space for service access. Check your space before ordering. The 120-pound weight requires floor reinforcement in some attics.
How much will this system reduce my monthly heating and cooling bills? +
Actual savings depend on climate and usage. In mixed climates, homeowners typically see 15 to 25 percent reductions versus 10-year-old equipment. The 96% AFUE furnace cuts heating costs significantly. The 14.5 SEER heat pump reduces cooling costs by 30 to 40 percent compared to baseline 10 SEER units.
Is the R454B refrigerant compatible with older copper line sets in my home? +
Yes, R454B works with standard copper piping. However, you cannot mix R454B with older R410A systems. This requires a full system replacement, not a retrofit. The factory includes 50 feet of pre-insulated suction line, reducing field fabrication.
What happens to my warranty if I use a contractor outside the ACiQ network? +
The 10-year parts coverage applies regardless of installer, provided you register online. Some ACiQ authorized dealers offer extended labor warranties, but that varies by location. Register immediately after installation to activate warranty terms.
