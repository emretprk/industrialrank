// Bottom Line
This ACiQ 2-ton horizontal unit is built for homeowners upgrading from older gas furnaces or replacing single-stage systems in moderate climates. The 96% AFUE heating efficiency and 14 SEER2 cooling make it a solid mid-tier choice for mixed-use heating and cooling applications where space constraints favor horizontal mounting.

Review

ACiQ's 2-ton horizontal gas furnace and heat pump system lands squarely in the practical mid-range tier that appeals to cost-conscious contractors and homeowners alike. The unit delivers 24,000 BTU heating and cooling capacity in a compact horizontal footprint (38.25 inches tall, 29 inches wide and deep), making it well-suited for attic and utility closet installations. ACiQ has earned contractor respect for straightforward engineering without unnecessary complexity, and this model reflects that philosophy with proven Goodman components and a scrolltype compressor.

Performance metrics matter here. The 96% AFUE furnace efficiency is genuinely strong for heating-dominated climates, translating to measurable utility savings over 80-85% baseline units. Cooling efficiency registers at 14 SEER2 with R454B refrigerant, which qualifies for ENERGY STAR certification and opens rebate eligibility in many jurisdictions. The variable-speed ECM blower motor adjusts output between 0 and 1,200 CFM, reducing fan electricity parasitic draw. Electrical requirements are modest: 17.1 to 25 amps on 208/230V single-phase power, standard for most residential panels.

Installation

Horizontal mounting requires careful planning for condensate drainage and air return paths. PVC exhaust venting simplifies installation compared to metal alternatives and works well with plastic duct systems common in newer construction. The 3/8-inch liquid line and 3/4-inch suction line with 3/4-inch insulation thickness demand properly sized and insulated lineset work. Sweat valve connections require brazing skill. Unit weight of 120 pounds is manageable for two-person installation on stable platforms.

Warranty & Reliability

ACiQ offers a 10-year comprehensive warranty with online registration required. This covers parts and labor defects, standard across mid-tier brands. Registration is straightforward and documented. The warranty does not cover wear items like capacitors or contractor-induced damage. Documentation includes full installation and service manuals, supporting long-term serviceability.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the actual cooling capacity, and how does 14 SEER2 compare to higher ratings? +
The unit delivers 24,000 BTU of cooling at single-stage full capacity. 14 SEER2 is respectable mid-range efficiency, roughly 15-20% below premium 18+ SEER2 models. It qualifies for rebates but won't deliver the lowest-cost cooling operation in high-use climates.
Can this horizontal unit fit in a standard attic, and what clearance is needed? +
Yes, at 38.25 inches tall and 29x29 inches width/depth, it fits most residential attics. Plan 12+ inches clearance above and around the unit for airflow and maintenance access. Joists must support 120 pounds securely. Ensure return air pathways are unobstructed.
Does the variable-speed blower really save energy compared to single-speed units? +
Yes, measurably. ECM motors consume 15-25% less blower electricity during part-load heating and cooling versus fixed-speed alternatives. Over a heating season, this translates to $50-150 annual savings depending on runtime and local electricity rates.
How does this ACiQ model compare to Goodman and Amana equivalents? +
ACiQ, Goodman, and Amana share parent company Daikin platforms and similar component sourcing. This unit mirrors Goodman GSZ140251 specifications. ACiQ offers slightly better pricing while sacrificing brand recognition. Reliability and warranty are functionally identical across these three.
What happens if I don't register the warranty online? +
The 10-year coverage applies only with online registration. Without it, warranty may default to a shorter period. Registration takes minutes and requires purchase documentation. Do this immediately after installation to protect your investment.
