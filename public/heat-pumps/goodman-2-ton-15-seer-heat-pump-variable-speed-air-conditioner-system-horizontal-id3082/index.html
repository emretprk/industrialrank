Review

The Goodman 2-ton heat pump system sits squarely in the practical middle market, offering real efficiency gains at an entry-level price point contractors appreciate. At $766, this unit undercuts premium brands while delivering genuine variable-speed technology. Energy Star certification confirms it meets EPA efficiency standards. The horizontal A-coil configuration suits typical attic and closet installations where space limits vertical units.

Performance specs show 24,000 BTU cooling and heating capacity with a 16 SEER1 rating, providing approximately 15 percent better efficiency than baseline 13-14 SEER systems. The variable-speed ECM blower motor maxes out at 1,200 CFM and adjusts runtime based on actual temperature demand. The scroll compressor operates on 208/230V single-phase 60 Hz power at 15-amp breaker sizing. R410A refrigerant compatibility aligns with current EPA standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the listing say 2-ton but specs show 3-ton capacity? +
This appears to be a data entry error on the product page. Actual capacity is 24,000 BTU (2-ton equivalent). Contact the seller to confirm tonnage before ordering. Many suppliers list equipment by both cooling and heating capacity, which can create confusion online.
Can I install this myself, or do I need a licensed HVAC tech? +
Most jurisdictions require licensed technicians for heat pump installation due to refrigerant handling regulations. The unit itself has straightforward bolt-down mounting, but evacuation, charging, and electrical connections demand EPA-certified expertise and proper equipment.
How much will this actually save on my electric bills compared to my old unit? +
A 16 SEER1 system uses approximately 15-20 percent less energy than 13 SEER equipment in cooling mode. Annual savings depend on your climate, usage patterns, and local electricity rates. In moderate climates, typical homeowners see $200-400 yearly reductions.
Should I buy this or the more expensive variable-capacity models? +
Single-stage units like this provide solid efficiency at part-load, but two-stage compressors deliver quieter operation and better performance during partial cooling demands. Choose this Goodman if budget is tight; upgrade if noise or extreme climate efficiency matters most.
Is the 10-year warranty automatic or do I have to register? +
Warranty requires online registration to activate the full 10 years. Without registration, coverage may be limited. Registration is quick and typically cost-free, protecting your investment across the compressor and major components.
