Review

The 4-ton Goodman heat pump system delivers 47,000 BTU for both heating and cooling, positioning itself as a mid-range solution for residential properties. Manufactured by Online Supply and carrying the SKU LS381183850, this unit bridges the gap between budget equipment and premium high-efficiency systems. The Energy Star certification signals compliance with EPA standards, and the two-stage condenser design reflects modern efficiency expectations. At this price point and specification level, it competes directly with mid-tier Lennox and Carrier offerings.

The 18 SEER1 rating represents solid efficiency, though contractors should note SEER2 ratings would be lower (typically 15-16 range). The variable-speed ECM blower motor is a standout feature, allowing the system to ramp down during mild weather instead of cycling on and off continuously. At 73 dB, sound levels are acceptable for most residential settings. The scroll compressor and two-stage configuration work together to reduce unnecessary runtime, translating to lower utility costs for homeowners in mixed-demand climates.

Installation

Installation requires standard sweat connections with 3/8-inch liquid line and 1 1/8-inch suction line, both straightforward for experienced technicians. The horizontal A-coil configuration takes up 24.5 inches high by 26 inches wide by 21.125 inches deep, fitting most attic installations. Electrical service needs either 208/230V single-phase 60 Hz power, and the breaker sizing range of 15 to 29.3 amps requires careful load calculations. Optional electric backup heat modules (3 to 20 kilowatts) add flexibility for cold climates.

Warranty & Reliability

The 10-year parts warranty activates upon online registration, providing strong protection for both contractors and end-users. Coverage extends to the compressor, which is typically the most expensive component to replace. Registration is straightforward and documentation is included in the installation manual. For commercial or rental properties, verify whether Online Supply transfers warranty to new owners.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this unit work in cold climates without the electric backup heat? +
The 47,000 BTU heating capacity handles moderate winters well, but regions below 20 degrees F regularly will benefit from optional electric backup heat modules. Available options range from 3 to 20 kilowatts depending on climate severity and property size. Consult your installer on sizing for your specific location.
Can I install this myself or do I need a licensed HVAC technician? +
EPA regulations require certified technicians to handle refrigerant work, and improper installation voids the 10-year warranty. Sweat connections and electrical work also demand professional expertise. Hire a licensed contractor to ensure proper charge, airflow, and safety compliance.
What makes the variable-speed ECM blower more efficient than standard motors? +
The variable-speed motor adjusts output from low to high capacity (up to 2,000 CFM) based on heating or cooling demand. During mild weather, it runs slower and uses less electricity. Standard single-speed motors run at full capacity regardless, wasting energy and increasing utility bills by 10 to 15 percent.
How does the 18 SEER rating compare to newer systems with higher SEER2 ratings? +
SEER1 and SEER2 use different test standards. An 18 SEER1 unit typically delivers 14 to 15 SEER2. Newer high-efficiency units claiming 20 SEER2 offer 5 to 10 percent better real-world performance, but at significantly higher cost. This unit provides good value.
What does the 10-year warranty actually cover? +
Parts coverage includes the compressor, condenser coils, and most internal components. Labor is typically not included. Registration with Online Supply is required within 30 days of installation. Read the full warranty document carefully before purchase.
