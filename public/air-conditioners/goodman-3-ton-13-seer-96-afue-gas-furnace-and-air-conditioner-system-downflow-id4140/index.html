Frequently Asked Questions

Will this unit replace my old Goodman GSX130362? +
Yes. The ACiQ-36-EHPD-UPG is a direct replacement for GSX130362, GSX130363, and GSX130365 units. Existing refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and ductwork typically require minimal adaptation. Always verify mounting compatibility with your specific cabinet before ordering.
What's the difference between 13 SEER and 13 SEER2? +
SEER2 uses updated testing standards that typically show 15 to 20 percent lower numbers than SEER1. This unit's 13 SEER1 rating is equivalent to roughly 10.5 to 11 SEER2. Most existing units were rated in SEER1, so direct comparisons require this conversion.
Is 96% AFUE good for a gas furnace? +
Yes. 96% AFUE is considered high-efficiency and represents the practical ceiling for non-condensing furnaces. It exceeds the federal minimum of 80% AFUE by a substantial margin, translating to approximately 16 percent lower heating bills versus baseline models.
Can I use this in an upflow position? +
The specs list upflow/downflow configuration capability, but ACiQ markets this as a downflow unit. Verify with technical documentation or manufacturer support before attempting upflow installation, as internal components may be optimized for gravity-assisted downflow operation.
Does online warranty registration cost anything? +
No. Goodman's online registration is free and takes approximately 5 minutes. Proof of installation may be required. Without registration, warranty coverage reverts to the standard five-year period.
