Review

The 2-ton Goodman gas furnace and air conditioner system occupies solid middle ground in the residential HVAC market. This horizontal configuration suits homes where upflow or horizontal mounting works with existing ductwork layout. At 96% AFUE for heating and 16 SEER2 for cooling, it targets contractors and homeowners who prioritize documented efficiency without reaching premium product tiers. Online Supply's SKU LS38343850 replaces the GSX16S241, making upgrades straightforward for existing installations.

Performance specs tell a straightforward story. The furnace delivers 80,000 BTU of heating output using a two-stage burner that reduces runtime cycling and improves comfort control. Cooling capacity reaches 23,600 BTU, appropriate for moderate climate zones and smaller homes or additions. The multi-speed ECM blower motor adjusts CFM up to 1200, matching seasonal demand. Operating at 71.5 dB keeps noise reasonable though not whisper-quiet. The scroll compressor and R410A refrigerant compatibility meet current environmental standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does 96% AFUE compare to my current furnace efficiency? +
Standard older furnaces operate at 78-85% AFUE. At 96%, this Goodman model converts 96 cents of every fuel dollar into usable heat, with only 4 cents lost up the flue. For a 60,000 annual heating degree-day climate, upgrading from 80% AFUE saves roughly $200-300 yearly on natural gas.
Can I install this in an existing 3-ton system location? +
Physically, yes, dimensions are 34.5 inches high by 17.5 inches wide by 28.75 inches deep. However, verify your cooling load actually requires only 2 tons (23,600 BTU). Oversizing or undersizing creates efficiency problems. Have a contractor perform a proper load calculation before replacing equipment.
Is 16 SEER2 good cooling efficiency for this tonnage? +
Yes. SEER2 testing uses realistic temperatures matching actual use. At 16 SEER2, this system ranks solid mid-tier cooling efficiency. Premium units reach 20-22 SEER2 but cost significantly more. For moderate climates or homes without extreme cooling demands, 16 SEER2 delivers genuine energy savings without premium pricing.
What separates this from cheaper Goodman alternatives? +
Lower-cost Goodman models use single-stage burners and 14-15 SEER2 cooling. This unit's two-stage burner and 16 SEER2 rating improve temperature control and cooling efficiency measurably. The ECM blower is standard here but absent on entry-level models, making monthly electricity use noticeably lower.
Does online registration really matter for warranty? +
Yes. Full 10-year parts coverage requires registration. Without it, coverage drops significantly. Registration takes five minutes online and protects you if a compressor or motor fails within decade. Goodman enforces this consistently.
