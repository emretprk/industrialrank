Review

ACiQ's 1.5 ton vertical cased coil occupies solid middle ground in the budget air conditioning market. This A-coil configuration addresses a genuine contractor pain point: finding quality evaporator coils that fit restrictive installation spaces without charging premium prices. The 18,000 BTU capacity and 14 SEER rating position it competitively against economy models while maintaining reliability credentials that matter in field applications.

Performance specs show this unit delivering 18,000 BTU cooling output with a rotary compressor running single-stage operation. The 14 SEER1 efficiency rating translates to approximately 12-15 percent better performance than 10 SEER baseline units from a decade ago, creating meaningful seasonal savings. Operating at 71 decibels places it in the audible range during peak cooling, a consideration for noise-sensitive installations. The R454B refrigerant aligns with industry forward movement away from R410A constraints.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this coil work with non-Goodman outdoor units? +
Yes, the standard valve connections (3/8 inch liquid, 3/4 inch suction) make it compatible with most outdoor condensing units rated 1.5 tons. Verify tonnage matching and refrigerant compatibility (R454B) before purchase, as mismatches create efficiency and reliability problems.
What does vertical 14 inch cased coil mean for installation? +
This A-coil orientation mounts vertically in tight spaces like attics or small closets where horizontal coils won't fit. The 14 inch width is narrower than standard 17-18 inch models, solving space constraints in older homes with limited HVAC room clearance.
How does 14 SEER compare to newer SEER2 ratings? +
SEER1 and SEER2 use different testing standards, making direct comparison difficult. Expect roughly 10-15 percent lower real-world performance under SEER2 methodology. For budget buyers, this 14 SEER rating still delivers solid efficiency gains over 10-12 SEER legacy equipment.
Why is the spec sheet confusing about tonnage (1.5 vs 3 ton)? +
The product title states 1.5 ton but specifications list 3 ton, likely a data entry error on the supplier's side. The 18,000 BTU cooling capacity definitively confirms 1.5 ton sizing. Contact the distributor for clarification before ordering to avoid system mismatch problems.
Does online warranty registration affect coverage? +
Yes, registration is required to activate the 10-year warranty protection. Complete registration immediately after installation with proof of purchase to secure coverage eligibility for potential future claims.
