Review

The ACiQ 2-ton air conditioner with horizontal slab coil fills a specific niche in the replacement market. Branded as a Goodman alternative, this unit delivers proven cooling capacity of 24,000 BTU paired with a 13 SEER rating that meets baseline efficiency expectations. The horizontal slab configuration addresses a real installation challenge, fitting into tight ceiling cavities and mechanical closets where standard vertical condensers simply won't fit.

Performance metrics show a single-stage rotary compressor operating on 208/230V single-phase power at 15-amp maximum breaker size. The 75 dB noise level reflects typical performance for a budget-tier unit, audible but not disruptive in most residential settings. R454B refrigerant aligns with current EPA standards, and the 24,000 BTU output matches the 2-ton specification precisely. Specs show liquid line at 3/8-inch and suction line at 3/4-inch with sweat valve connections.

Installation

The 50-foot pre-insulated lineset eliminates field fabrication, cutting installation labor significantly. Sweat valve connections require soldering, which demands certified technician experience. The 120-pound weight and 25x25.5x12-inch footprint make handling straightforward for two technicians. Most installations complete 30 to 40 percent faster than units requiring lineset fabrication and field insulation wrapping.

Warranty & Reliability

The 10-year parts warranty requires online registration to activate. This covers compressor, refrigerant circuit components, and the coil itself. Registration takes five minutes and must happen within 90 days of purchase. No labor coverage is included, so budget separately for service calls if repairs become necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a direct replacement for Goodman GSX130242 systems? +
Yes, the SKU ACIQ-24-EHPD-UPG replaces GSX130242 units exactly. Same 24,000 BTU capacity, identical voltage requirements (208/230V single-phase), matching lineset configuration, and compatible refrigerant type. Mounting and electrical connections transfer without modification.
What makes the 50-foot lineset so valuable for installation? +
Pre-insulated linesets eliminate field purchases and labor-intensive wrapping with foam insulation. The 3/4-inch insulation thickness prevents condensation and heat loss. Contractors avoid specialty tool requirements and installation delays, completing jobs one day faster in typical residential settings.
How does 13 SEER compare to newer air conditioner standards? +
13 SEER meets minimum federal requirements but trails higher-efficiency models rated 15 to 18 SEER. For moderate climates with 500 to 1,000 cooling hours annually, this unit delivers acceptable operating costs. High-heat regions justify upgrading for long-term energy savings.
Why choose this over other 2-ton replacement options? +
The horizontal slab design solves installation constraints vertical units cannot address. Pre-insulated lineset cuts labor costs significantly. GSX130242 compatibility makes replacements seamless. Price efficiency makes sense for budget-conscious replacements without premium requirements.
Does the 10-year warranty require annual maintenance records? +
No maintenance documentation is required. Online registration within 90 days activates the 10-year coverage. Proof of purchase is helpful but not mandatory. Warranty covers defects and refrigerant leaks from manufacturing flaws only, not wear items.
