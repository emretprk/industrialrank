Installation

Professional installation remains essential, typically requiring 1-2 days for standard split systems and 4-6 hours for ductless mini-splits. Technicians need access to interior walls for line-set routing and outdoor space for condenser placement. Electrical upgrades to 240-volt 60-amp service may be necessary, adding $800-1,200 to total project cost. Licensed contractors should verify local code compliance and refrigerant handling certifications.

Warranty & Reliability

Manufacturers offer tiered protection including 10-12 year compressor warranties, 5-10 year parts coverage, and 5-year labor warranties when installed by certified contractors. Some brands extend 10-year coverage on heat exchangers and coils, covering failure points that typically emerge after year five. Warranty transferability to second owners adds resale value, though coverage often drops to 5 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

What real-world savings can I expect from a SEER2 20 unit versus older equipment? +
A home running cooling 2,000 hours annually at $0.14/kWh sees approximately $480-600 yearly savings compared to 13 SEER equipment. Over a 15-year lifespan, cumulative savings reach $7,200-9,000 before accounting for potential rate increases. Humid climates with higher runtime extend payback advantages significantly.
Do these brands work with existing ductwork, or do I need new ducts? +
Existing ductwork works if sized correctly, typically 0.6-1 inch static pressure drop. Undersized ducts reduce efficiency by 15-20 percent and void some warranty provisions. Professional duct testing costs $150-300 but ensures your investment performs as rated.
How do variable-speed compressors improve performance? +
Variable-capacity units ramp output from 25-100 percent based on demand, eliminating the constant on-off cycling of single-stage compressors. This reduces temperature swings by 1-2 degrees, cuts compressor stress, and improves efficiency during partial-load conditions when most cooling occurs. Lifespan increases 3-5 years typically.
Are these brands better than mid-tier alternatives costing 30-40 percent less? +
Premium brands offer superior reliability, 2-3 year longer lifespan, and 1-2 SEER2 points higher efficiency. Mid-tier equipment satisfies most homeowners and recovers faster on modest usage. Premium brands justify cost primarily through extended durability and minimized downtime risk.
What maintenance do I need to keep the warranty active? +
Annual professional service is required, including filter changes, refrigerant checks, and coil cleaning. Many brands mandate service logs for compressor claims. DIY maintenance like filter replacement every 30-90 days is permitted and recommended for maintaining rated efficiency and system longevity.
