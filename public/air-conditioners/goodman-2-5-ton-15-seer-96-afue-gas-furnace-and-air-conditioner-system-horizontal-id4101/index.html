Review

The Goodman 2.5 Ton Gas Furnace and Air Conditioner System represents a balanced approach to residential HVAC replacement. At $115, this horizontal configuration targets contractors and homeowners seeking efficient comfort without premium brand pricing. The system combines a 40,000 BTU furnace with a 29,000 BTU air conditioner, designed for typical single-family homes. Goodman's reputation for durability at accessible price points makes this unit particularly attractive for regional contractors managing multiple installations annually.

Efficiency is this system's strongest selling point. The furnace delivers 96% AFUE, meaning nearly all fuel converts to usable heat, qualifying buyers for state and utility incentives. The air conditioner achieves 16 SEER cooling efficiency, surpassing ENERGY STAR minimums. The variable-speed ECM blower motor intelligently adjusts fan speed based on heating or cooling demand, reducing runtime and electrical consumption. Two-stage burner operation provides low-fire capability during mild weather, further conserving natural gas or propane fuel. These specifications position the unit among the more efficient options at this price tier.

Installation

This horizontal unit suits attic, crawlspace, or utility closet installations where vertical space is limited. Standard 115V single-phase power with 17.1 to 25 amp breaker requirements keeps electrical upgrades minimal. The plastic PVC exhaust requirement simplifies venting compared to metal flue alternatives. Scroll compressor design and standard 3/8 inch liquid and 7/8 inch suction lines follow industry norms, reducing special tooling needs. Installation time typically mirrors standard furnace replacements for experienced technicians.

Warranty & Reliability

Goodman provides a 10-year parts and labor warranty upon online registration, covering compressor, heat exchanger, and coil failures. This length matches industry mid-range offerings but falls short of premium manufacturer extended warranties. Registration is mandatory, so documentation becomes critical. The warranty excludes improper installation, refrigerant leaks from neglected maintenance, and damage from power surges or external factors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this system support both R410A and R22 refrigerant? +
Yes, the specs indicate compatibility with both R410A and R22 refrigerants. However, verify with your installer which refrigerant is charged, as mixing is prohibited. R410A is standard for new systems, while R22 is phasing out. Confirm before purchase to ensure parts availability in your region.
Can I install this in a horizontal attic space without modifications? +
Absolutely. The horizontal configuration with 21.125 inch height and 26 inch depth fits most attic joists. Plastic PVC exhaust venting simplifies installation through walls or roofs. Ensure adequate return air access and clearance around the unit for service access. Your installer should verify your attic framing can support the 30-pound weight.
How does 16 SEER cooling compare to other units in this price range? +
16 SEER is solid mid-range performance, exceeding basic ENERGY STAR thresholds and qualifying for many utility rebates. However, variable-capacity units achieve 18+ SEER through modulation. This single-stage compressor runs full-capacity when cooling, making it less efficient during mild weather operation compared to inverter-driven competitors.
What's the difference between the listed 2.5 and 3 ton specifications? +
The specs show conflicting information, listing both 2.5 and 3 ton capacity. The 29,000 BTU cooling output calculates to 2.4 tons, and 40,000 BTU heating is approximately 3.3 tons. Request clarification from the supplier before purchase to confirm actual capacity matches your home's load calculation.
Is the 10-year warranty enough protection? +
10 years covers most compressor failure risks during peak operating years. However, extended warranties available through dealers can protect beyond year 10. Online registration is required to activate coverage, so save documentation immediately after installation.
