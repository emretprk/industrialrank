Frequently Asked Questions

Can this unit directly replace my Goodman GSX130242? +
Yes, it's designed as a direct replacement with matching electrical specifications and coil dimensions. Verify your indoor evaporator coil is compatible Goodman equipment, as ACiQ components must pair with proper counterparts. Consult installation manual before ordering to confirm system compatibility.
What electrical work is required to install this unit? +
Standard 208/230V single-phase 60 Hz service with a breaker rated between 10.3 and 15 amps. Most homes with existing 2-ton units already have adequate electrical infrastructure. A licensed electrician should verify panel capacity and confirm proper breaker sizing before installation.
How does 13 SEER compare to newer high-efficiency units? +
13 SEER is baseline modern efficiency, roughly 20 to 30 percent less efficient than 16 to 18 SEER systems. You'll notice lower utility bills versus older units, but premium models cut energy costs further. Best for budget-conscious buyers or homes where efficiency gains won't justify higher upfront cost.
Is R454B refrigerant a good choice compared to R410A? +
R454B is superior environmentally with lower global warming potential, addressing EPA phase-out plans. It performs comparably to R410A in residential cooling. Technicians need proper certification and recovery equipment, so ensure your service provider is trained on newer refrigerant standards.
What does the 10-year warranty actually cover? +
Compressor and component failures due to manufacturing defects are covered. Online registration is required to activate. Normal wear, improper installation, and system negligence are excluded. Contact ACiQ with serial numbers and documentation for claim processing.
