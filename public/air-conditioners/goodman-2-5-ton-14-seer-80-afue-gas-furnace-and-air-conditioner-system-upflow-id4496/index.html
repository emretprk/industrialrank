Review

Goodman's 2.5 ton gas furnace and air conditioner system is a workhorse combination aimed at contractors and homeowners seeking reliable performance without premium pricing. Manufactured by Online Supply under SKU LS38343850, this upflow unit pairs an 80,000 BTU furnace with a 30,000 BTU condenser to handle moderate heating and cooling loads. It's the kind of system you see on job sites regularly, proven through thousands of installations across North America.

The furnace delivers 80% AFUE efficiency with a two-stage burner, meaningfully better than 78% single-stage alternatives. The AC side rates 14 SEER using a rotary compressor and single-stage condenser, hitting mid-market cooling efficiency. The variable-speed blower motor reaches 2,000 CFM maximum airflow, allowing better comfort control than fixed-speed designs. It runs at 72 dB, which is typical for this class but worth noting if noise matters to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does 80% AFUE compare to high-efficiency furnaces on monthly gas bills? +
An 80% AFUE furnace converts 80 cents of every heating dollar to usable heat versus 95%+ models converting 95 cents. Over a typical heating season, you'll spend 15-20% more on gas than with 95% AFUE equipment. For a $1,200 annual heating bill, expect roughly $180-240 higher costs annually.
Can this unit handle extreme climates or just moderate zones? +
The 80,000 BTU output works well in HVAC zones 4-6 (moderate to cold). In zone 7 or colder climates, you may need auxiliary electric heat or oversized ductwork. Consult local load calculations before committing to this tonnage.
Why is the compressor single-stage if the burner is two-stage? +
Single-stage compressors run full capacity or off, simpler and cheaper than variable units but less efficient on partial loads. Two-stage burners reduce short cycling during mild weather, while AC still cycles on-off. This design balances cost with meaningful heating efficiency gains.
How does 14 SEER compare to competitors at this price point? +
14 SEER is competitive for entry-level systems. Mid-tier units often hit 16-18 SEER with variable-capacity compressors costing $3,500-4,500 installed. This Goodman prioritizes affordability over peak efficiency, suitable for budget-conscious buyers.
Does online registration really matter for the 10-year warranty? +
Yes. Without registration, Goodman limits warranty to 5 years parts. Registration is free and takes 5 minutes online. Always register immediately after installation to unlock full coverage.
