Review

The Goodman 3.5/5-ton horizontal system bridges practical heating and cooling needs for residential installations across diverse fuel environments. Combining a 120,000 BTU furnace with a 42,000 BTU cooling capacity, this unit operates on propane or natural gas while delivering ENERGY STAR certification. Online Supply positions this as a mid-market option for contractors and homeowners seeking dependable performance without premium pricing, backed by Goodman's established service network and documented reliability history.

Heating efficiency reaches 96% AFUE through a two-stage burner that modulates output based on demand, reducing cycling losses. The air conditioner's 16 SEER1 rating and R410A refrigerant compatibility exceed baseline efficiency standards, qualifying buyers for regional utility rebates. The variable-speed ECM blower automatically adjusts airflow between 0-2000 CFM, minimizing energy waste during partial-load operation while maintaining consistent comfort across 120-square-foot to 150-square-foot spaces.

Pros & Cons

Installation

This horizontal A-coil configuration mounts in attics or crawlspaces with 50 feet of suction line insulation (0.5 inch thickness). Requires 115V single-phase power and 23.5 to 40-amp breaker sizing. PVC or plastic exhaust flue compatibility simplifies venting. Most installations take 4-6 hours for experienced technicians, assuming ducting already exists.

Warranty & Reliability

Goodman provides a lifetime warranty on the heat exchanger (major component protection) and 10-year parts coverage with online registration. This strong heat exchanger guarantee reflects confidence in build quality, though registration is mandatory to activate it. Typical labor remains customer responsibility, which is standard in the market.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between the 3.5-ton and 5-ton ratings listed? +
The specs sheet lists 5-ton tonnage with 42,000 BTU cooling capacity, which aligns with approximately 3.5-3.75 tons. This discrepancy suggests either a data entry error or the product title reflects a different configuration. Verify the AHRI certificate 7368842 with your distributor before ordering.
Can this handle both natural gas and propane without modifications? +
Yes, the unit supports both fuels, but it must be configured for one at installation. Conversion requires factory-authorized service and orifice changes. Plan fuel type selection before shipment to avoid costly field modifications.
How much will this reduce my energy bills compared to my 20-year-old system? +
Heating savings typically reach 30-35% over 80% AFUE units; cooling saves 20-25% versus 13 SEER systems. Exact gains depend on climate, usage patterns, and ductwork quality. Southern climates see larger cooling savings, northern regions benefit more from furnace efficiency.
Is 73 dB noise level acceptable for a bedroom attic installation? +
73 dB equals busy traffic volume. Bedroom installations benefit from sound damping or placement away from occupied rooms. Premium Goodman models offer quieter compressors, though this single-stage unit is typical for this price range.
What does online registration actually require for the lifetime heat exchanger warranty? +
Registration typically involves providing proof of purchase, installation date, and system serial number through Goodman's portal. Keep all paperwork. Failure to register within the specified timeframe voids the extended protection, so complete this immediately after installation.
