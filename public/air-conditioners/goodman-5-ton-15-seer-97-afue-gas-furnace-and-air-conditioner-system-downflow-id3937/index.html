Review

The Goodman 5-ton split system pairs a 100,000 BTU furnace with variable-speed cooling in a downflow configuration suitable for basements and closeted installations. Positioned as a value-conscious upgrade, this unit bridges economy and premium segments by delivering genuine efficiency improvements without flagship pricing. Energy Star certification and Goodman's established market presence make it approachable for both DIY-curious homeowners and professional installers navigating budget constraints.

This system delivers impressive heating efficiency at 97% AFUE, meaning 97 cents of every dollar directly heats your home. The cooler achieves 16 SEER1 rating, exceeding the listed 15 SEER specification and providing roughly 6-8% better cooling efficiency than baseline equipment. Modulating burner stages and two-stage scroll compressor operation reduce energy consumption during partial-load conditions, the reality for most homes 70% of operating hours. The variable-speed blower adjusts from 0 to 2000 CFM, matching output to actual heating and cooling demand.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Modulating furnace burner maintains precise temperatures, reducing energy waste and improving comfort compared to single-stage units
  • 16 SEER cooling efficiency significantly outperforms 15 SEER baseline, saving approximately 6-8% annually on cooling costs
  • Variable-speed ECM blower motor adjusts airflow from 0 to 2000 CFM, optimizing both efficiency and noise levels
  • Two-stage scroll compressor provides quieter operation at 75 dBA and better part-load efficiency
  • PVC exhaust flue compatibility simplifies installation and reduces corrosion concerns versus metal venting

Installation

This downflow unit requires 30 by 21 by 21 inch space and plastic PVC venting, simplifying ductwork modifications. Electrical demands fall between 37.2 and 60 amp breaker sizing, potentially necessitating panel evaluation and upgrade costs. Sweat valve connections (3/8 inch liquid, 7/8 inch suction lines) require competent refrigeration work or professional assistance. Installation complexity rates moderate for experienced technicians, higher for homeowners lacking HVAC experience.

Warranty & Reliability

Goodman provides 10 years parts coverage upon online registration, a respectable mid-tier commitment. This covers compressor, heat exchanger, and sealed components but excludes wear items and labor. Registration is mandatory, so delays void coverage. For a $0 system, this warranty stands as meaningful protection against catastrophic failures during the critical first decade.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the spec sheet list 60,000 BTU cooling for a 5-ton unit? +
A 5-ton system nominally handles 60,000 BTU/hour at peak cooling. The tonnage and BTU figures should align. Verify AHRI certificate 7424272 documentation before purchase to confirm actual capacity specifications and ensure sizing matches your home's cooling load requirements.
Will this system fit my basement installation? +
Yes, the downflow configuration works well for basements. Dimensions measure 30 inches tall by 21 by 21 inches wide and deep, fitting standard utility closets. Ensure 12-18 inches clearance above and around the unit for maintenance access and proper airflow distribution.
How much will my energy bills drop compared to a 13 SEER system? +
At 16 SEER versus 13 SEER cooling, expect approximately 18-20% cooling energy savings, translating to $200-400 annually depending on climate and usage. The 97% AFUE furnace saves similarly on heating, making total yearly HVAC savings substantial over the system's 15-20 year lifespan.
How does this compare to higher-priced Goodman or Lennox alternatives? +
This model sacrifices ultra-quiet operation, higher SEER ratings (18+), and extended warranties that premium units offer. However, at $0 listed price, the efficiency-to-cost ratio remains compelling for budget-conscious buyers who prioritize proven performance over cutting-edge features or brand prestige.
What does the 10-year warranty actually cover? +
Parts coverage includes compressor, heat exchanger, and sealed refrigeration components. Labor, ductwork, and electrical work are excluded. Online registration within 30 days of installation is mandatory. Contact Goodman for specific component limitations before purchase.
