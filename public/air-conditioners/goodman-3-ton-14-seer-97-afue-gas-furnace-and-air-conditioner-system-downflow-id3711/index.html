Review

The Goodman 3-ton downflow furnace and air conditioner combo represents solid value in the mid-range HVAC market. Goodman has built a reputation as a dependable workhorse brand, and this Online Supply SKU LS38783850 sits at an attractive $643 price point. The downflow A-coil configuration makes it flexible for attic, basement, or closet installations in single-story homes. This isn't luxury equipment, but it delivers genuine efficiency improvements over equipment from 2008-2012 era systems.

The numbers tell a practical story. The furnace achieves 97% AFUE with a modulating burner that adjusts flame height rather than running full-blast constantly. On the cooling side, the 16 SEER2 rating and 34,800 BTU capacity cover 3-ton cooling loads. The variable-speed ECM blower motor runs at different speeds based on demand rather than fixed high-low cycling. Together, these features should cut heating and cooling costs 20-30% compared to single-stage baseline equipment from 15 years ago.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 97% AFUE compare to my current furnace? +
If your existing furnace is 80-85% AFUE (typical for 2008-era equipment), this Goodman captures 12-17% more heat energy. On a $1,200 annual heating bill, expect savings of $144-204 per year, recovering the equipment cost premium within 4-6 years before considering cooling efficiency gains.
Does this system work with my existing ductwork? +
The downflow configuration routes air downward, so verify your ductwork exits below the unit. Maximum CFM of 1,200 fits standard residential ductwork sizes. You may need duct cleaning if your system is 15+ years old, as dirt buildup reduces the modulating burner's comfort benefits.
What's the difference between SEER and SEER2 ratings? +
SEER2 is the newer test standard that uses more realistic temperature conditions. The 16 SEER2 rating on this unit roughly equals 19-20 old-spec SEER numbers. Don't compare SEER2 directly to SEER ratings. For context, 16 SEER2 beats most single-stage equipment and qualifies for Energy Star rebates nationwide.
Is Goodman equipment reliable compared to Carrier or Lennox? +
Goodman uses the same scroll compressor and core components as higher-priced brands. Real-world failure rates are similar when properly installed and maintained. The main difference is warranty length, customer service, and cabinet materials, not core reliability. This unit's track record matches other brands in its price range.
What does the 10-year warranty actually cover? +
Parts only, including compressor and heat exchanger. Labor is excluded. Registration is required. Most failures occur in years 2-5. After year 10, major repairs likely cost more than replacement, so this warranty protects your investment during the critical period.
