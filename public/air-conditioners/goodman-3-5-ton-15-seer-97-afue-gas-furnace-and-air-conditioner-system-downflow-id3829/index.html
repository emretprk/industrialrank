// Bottom Line
This Goodman system excels for homeowners prioritizing heating efficiency and quiet operation in downflow installations. The 97% AFUE rating and modulating burner deliver measurable energy savings, making it ideal for cold climates where furnace runtime is extended.

Review

Goodman positions this 97% AFUE furnace as a bridge product between budget and premium HVAC systems. The combination of high-efficiency heating, solid 16 SEER2 cooling, and modulating burner technology addresses the core needs of contractors working in cost-conscious residential markets. Online Supply backs this unit with a 10-year warranty, reflecting confidence in component longevity and scroll compressor reliability.

Performance metrics tell an encouraging story. The furnace delivers 100,000 BTU heating with modulating burner technology, reducing energy waste during partial-load operation. The 42,000 BTU air conditioner achieves 16 SEER2, surpassing ENERGY STAR minimums. Variable-speed ECM blower adjusts from 0 to 2000 CFM, matching airflow to heating or cooling demand. At 73 dBA, noise remains acceptable for residential installation, though single-stage cooling means the compressor cycles rather than modulates capacity.

Who Should Buy

Best For

Contractors and homeowners in northern climates needing downflow configuration with strong heating output will appreciate the 100,000 BTU furnace and high modulation capability. Budget-conscious upgraders from older 80-85% AFUE systems will see tangible utility bill reductions within the first year.

Consider Alternatives If

Buyers seeking the quietest operation or living in mild climates with minimal heating needs should explore variable-capacity air conditioners. Those with existing three-phase electrical service or upflow-only space constraints should skip this downflow-specific model.

Installation

Downflow configuration demands careful ductwork planning, as return air must flow from above the unit. Technicians need 115V single-phase service with 23.5 to 40 amp breaker, standard in most homes. The 30 by 21 by 21 inch footprint fits basement and closet spaces. Plastic PVC exhaust flue simplifies venting compared to metal alternatives. Refrigerant lines use 3/8 inch liquid and 1 1/8 inch suction with 1/2 inch insulation, supporting runs to 50 feet.

Warranty & Reliability

The 10-year parts and labor warranty, activated through online registration, provides solid protection against compressor and furnace failure. Coverage begins at purchase and transfers to second owners. This exceeds typical 5-year limits on scroll compressors. Registration is straightforward and strongly recommended to avoid voiding coverage. Evaporator coil carries separate warranty documentation included in the resource package.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the spec sheet list both 3.5 ton and 5 ton capacity? +
The discrepancy appears to be a data entry error between the product title and specification table. The 42,000 BTU cooling output aligns with 3.5-ton capacity, not 5-ton. Confirm actual tonnage with the seller before ordering to ensure proper system sizing and compatibility with existing ductwork and linesets.
Can this downflow unit be installed in an upflow position? +
The configuration field lists Upflow/Downflow capability, but the product title emphasizes downflow. Contact the manufacturer to confirm A-coil orientation flexibility. Most A-coils work in both directions, but airflow ducting and condensate drain routing differ significantly between configurations.
How much will heating costs drop compared to an 80% AFUE furnace? +
A 97% AFUE unit wastes only 3% of fuel energy versus 20% waste in 80% units. On a $1,200 annual heating bill, you'll recover approximately $200 yearly. Payback period depends on local gas rates and climate, but northern homeowners typically break even within 4 to 5 years.
Does this system compete with Lennox or Carrier mid-tier models? +
In efficiency, yes. The 16 SEER2 cooling and 97% AFUE heating match Carrier Performance and Lennox XC offerings. However, Goodman's single-stage compressor lacks the variable modulation those brands offer, meaning more on-off cycling during partial loads. Goodman wins on price, loses slightly on comfort and noise.
What does the 10-year warranty actually cover? +
Parts and labor on compressor, furnace heat exchanger, and blower motor are covered. Registration through Online Supply activates coverage. Evaporator coil has separate warranty terms. Read the included documentation carefully, as some components may carry shorter terms or exclude wear items.
