Review

The ACiQ 1.5 Ton condenser with vertical 21-inch uncased coil represents practical, no-nonsense cooling for the residential replacement market. This unit sits at the efficiency baseline where federal standards and real-world budgets intersect. Contractors trust it for straightforward installations, while homeowners appreciate the straightforward performance without premium pricing. The spec sheet reveals a competent piece of equipment designed for reliability over flashy features.

Performance specs tell the story here. The unit delivers 18,000 BTU of cooling capacity with 13 SEER1 efficiency, positioning it squarely in the baseline tier. Frequently Asked Questions

How does 13 SEER1 compare to modern high-efficiency units? +
13 SEER1 meets the federal minimum standard set in 2023 but lags 16-20 SEER units by roughly 20-30 percent in seasonal efficiency. You'll see this difference on utility bills over 15-20 years. Choose this for budget projects, not maximum long-term savings.
Can I pair this with any Goodman evaporator coil? +
Yes, but verify tonnage matching. This 1.5-ton unit pairs with 1.5-ton coils for optimal performance. Mismatched tonnage creates refrigerant distribution issues and reduced cooling capacity. Check the coil specification sheet before purchase.
What does R454B refrigerant mean for my system? +
R454B is the EPA-approved drop-in replacement for R410A, reducing environmental impact while maintaining compatibility with existing piping and components. It ensures your system remains compliant through 2030 and beyond, protecting your investment.
Is this unit quieter than older R410A condensers? +
No, at 74 dB this runs louder than premium units but similar to budget-tier competitors. For perspective, normal conversation measures 60 dB. Place it away from bedrooms and patios if noise matters in your application.
Does the 10-year warranty cover labor and refrigerant? +
No, the warranty covers manufacturer defects in materials only. Labor and refrigerant charges fall on the homeowner or contractor. Review the full warranty document after registration for specific coverage exclusions.
