Review

The Goodman 2-ton 15 SEER system represents solid mid-market positioning in the 2023-2024 HVAC replacement cycle. At $643, this upflow-configured package combines a 60,000 BTU modulating gas furnace with a 24,000 BTU air conditioning condenser, addressing both seasonal demands with a single installation footprint. Goodman's reputation for straightforward equipment without unnecessary complexity appeals to cost-conscious contractors and homeowners managing tight replacement budgets.

The 97% AFUE furnace with modulating burners intelligently ramps heat output between minimum and full capacity, reducing temperature swings and minimizing short-cycle losses. The air handler features a variable-speed ECM blower reaching 1200 CFM maximum, adapting to actual cooling or heating demand rather than running full blast continuously. Energy Star certification confirms the system meets EPA efficiency thresholds, though the SEER1 rating of 14 sits below premium competitors hitting 16-18 SEER performance levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 97% AFUE compare to older furnace efficiency? +
A 97% AFUE furnace converts 97 cents of every fuel dollar into usable heat, versus 80-85% in equipment from 2005-2010. On a $1,200 annual heating bill, you recover roughly $180-200 annually through efficiency gains, offsetting upgrade costs within 3-4 years for most climates.
Can this unit work with existing ductwork from my old system? +
Usually yes, provided your ducts accommodate 1200 CFM maximum airflow without excessive restriction. Undersized ductwork may reduce cooling efficiency or cause static pressure issues. A qualified installer measures existing duct sizing to confirm compatibility before final connection.
Why is single-stage cooling a limitation? +
Single-stage compressors run at full capacity or off, consuming maximum power even during mild 60-70 degree weather. Two-stage or modulating compressors reduce capacity proportionally, cutting energy use 20-30% during partial-load conditions typical in spring and fall.
How does R454B refrigerant affect future servicing? +
R454B is EPA-approved and widely adopted, ensuring technician availability and parts supply through 2040. Older R-22 systems become increasingly expensive to service as supplies shrink, making R454B a future-proof choice for 10-15 year equipment lifespan.
Is the 10-year warranty worth the online registration step? +
Yes, registration takes five minutes and formally documents your purchase date, preventing disputes over coverage eligibility. Keep the registration confirmation email for warranty claims, as manufacturers require proof of purchase date and model verification.
