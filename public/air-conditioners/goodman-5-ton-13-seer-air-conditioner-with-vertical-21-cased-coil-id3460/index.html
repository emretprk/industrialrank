Review

The ACiQ 5-ton vertical cased coil fills a practical niche in the retrofit market, particularly for contractors replacing discontinued Goodman models GSX130603 through GSX130614. This A-style coil handles both upflow and downflow configurations, giving installers flexibility on tight job sites. At 120 pounds and compact 21 by 21 inch footprint, it's designed for straightforward vertical installations without extensive ductwork modifications.

Performance specs show 60,000 BTU cooling capacity paired with a 13 SEER1 efficiency rating, positioning it as a competent mid-market choice. The reciprocating compressor runs on 208/230V single-phase power with a 32.8 to 45 amp breaker range, standard for residential applications. R454B refrigerant ensures compatibility with modern equipment while supporting environmental compliance standards across most jurisdictions.

Installation

Installation connects via 3/8 inch sweat fittings for both liquid and suction lines, using standard hand tools and brazing equipment. The 30-foot pre-insulated suction line with 3/8 inch insulation thickness minimizes field preparation. Upflow or downflow orientation adapts to existing ductwork configuration, making this coil accessible for experienced technicians or professional installers.

Warranty & Reliability

The 10-year parts warranty activates through online registration, providing comprehensive coverage for manufacturing defects. ACiQ handles compressor and component replacement at no cost during this period, though homeowners must maintain proper maintenance records. This warranty duration matches industry standards for aftermarket coils, offering reasonable protection for budget-conscious buyers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this coil compatible with my existing Goodman outdoor unit? +
Yes, it directly replaces Goodman models GSX130603, GSX130604, GSX130605, GSX130611, GSX130613, and GSX130614. Verify your outdoor unit model before ordering. R454B refrigerant compatibility with your condenser is essential, so confirm with manufacturer specifications before installation.
What electrical requirements do I need for installation? +
This unit requires 208V or 230V single-phase 60Hz power with a breaker sized between 32.8 and 45 amps. Most residential homes accommodate this voltage without modifications. Have a licensed electrician verify existing panel capacity and install appropriate breaker sizing for safe operation.
How does 13 SEER1 compare to newer high-efficiency models? +
13 SEER1 represents solid baseline cooling efficiency, approximately 15 to 25 percent lower than premium 16+ SEER inverter-driven systems. For moderate climate zones with moderate cooling demand, this efficiency level balances performance with reasonable operating costs.
Can I use this coil with my non-Goodman outdoor condenser? +
Cross-brand compatibility depends on matching refrigerant type, tonnage rating, and connection fittings. While some contractors mix brands successfully, ACiQ and Goodman alignment is most reliable. Consult technical specifications or call manufacturer support before mixing equipment.
Does the 10-year warranty cover refrigerant recharge costs? +
The warranty covers parts replacement only, not refrigerant, labor, or service calls. Homeowners must handle recurring maintenance costs separately. Online registration is required to activate coverage within 30 days of installation.
