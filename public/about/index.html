What We Do

Industrial equipment procurement is expensive and high-stakes. A wrong HVAC specification for a 100,000 square foot facility can mean years of excess energy costs and expensive retrofits. A circuit breaker that fails under fault conditions costs far more than the equipment itself. A pump seal failure in a chemical process line means downtime, contamination risk, and emergency contractor calls.

IndustrialRank was built to give facility managers, engineers, and procurement teams a faster path to better decisions. We compile manufacturer specifications, third-party test data, field reports, and real-world application data into structured reviews that answer the questions buyers actually ask: which unit performs best at partial load, which breaker holds up in high-humidity environments, which pump seal design has the lowest failure rate in chemical service.

We are not a manufacturer. We do not build equipment, represent any brand, or take direction from any supplier on our editorial coverage. Our revenue comes from affiliate commissions when readers purchase products we review, which means we are financially motivated to recommend products that actually satisfy buyers, not products that pay the highest referral rate.

Our Review Methodology

Every review on IndustrialRank is built from a defined set of sources. We do not publish reviews based on general impressions or marketing materials alone. Our standard data stack for each review includes the following.

Manufacturer Specifications

Published performance data, AHRI certifications, UL listings, and technical data sheets from the manufacturer. We cite the specification version and date for every review.

Third-Party Test Data

AHRI certification database, DOE efficiency testing results, independent laboratory evaluations, and utility program qualification data where available.

Field Reports

Documented installation reports, contractor feedback, facility manager accounts, and distributor service data from the field. We cross-reference multiple sources before incorporating field data into a review.

Comparative Analysis

Head-to-head specification comparison against direct competitors in the same class, price range, and application category. Every review identifies the conditions under which a competing product is the better choice.

Total Cost of Ownership

Where sufficient data exists, we model TCO across a 10 to 15 year service life, incorporating energy costs, maintenance intervals, expected failure modes, and replacement part availability.

Application Fit Analysis

Every review explicitly identifies the facility types, climate conditions, load profiles, and operational contexts where a product performs best and worst. We do not recommend products without application context.

Editorial Independence

IndustrialRank does not accept payment from manufacturers to influence editorial coverage, ranking positions, or review outcomes. Manufacturers cannot purchase favorable reviews, request removal of unfavorable content, or negotiate rating scores.

We participate in affiliate programs including those operated through Commission Junction. When a reader purchases a product through a link on this site, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to the buyer. This commission structure does not influence which products we review, how we score them, or what conclusions we reach. Our editorial process is completed before affiliate link placement is considered.

If a product we review does not have an available affiliate link, we review it anyway if it is relevant to our coverage areas. Many of the products we recommend most strongly are not available through affiliate programs at all.

Coverage Areas

HVAC Systems

Commercial and industrial heating, ventilation, and air conditioning equipment including variable refrigerant flow systems, heat pumps, rooftop units, chillers, air handlers, and controls. We cover equipment from 2-ton residential edge cases through large commercial tonnage. Our HVAC coverage emphasizes energy efficiency, cold-climate performance, humidity control, and total cost of ownership.

Electrical Equipment

Industrial and commercial electrical supply equipment including circuit breakers, switchgear, motor control centers, arc flash protection systems, EV charging infrastructure, and panel components. We cover both standard and specialized industrial applications, with particular attention to high-humidity environments, high-fault-current service, and NFPA 70E compliance requirements.

Industrial Pumps

Centrifugal, magnetic drive, submersible, diaphragm, and multistage pumps for commercial and industrial applications. Our pump coverage emphasizes chemical compatibility, seal reliability, variable speed operation, and predictive maintenance integration. We cover pump selection for HVAC, chemical process, water treatment, and general industrial service.
