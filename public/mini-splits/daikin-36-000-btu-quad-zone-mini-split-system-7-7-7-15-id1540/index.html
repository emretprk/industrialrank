Daikin's reputation for reliability translates here, backed by AHRI certificates 205663360, 205663361, and 205663368 confirming tested performance across multiple conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does 18 SEER2 actually mean for monthly cooling costs? +
SEER2 measures seasonal cooling efficiency under real-world conditions. An 18 SEER2 unit uses approximately 25 percent less energy than 15 SEER equipment over a cooling season, translating to $300-600 annual savings in hot climates depending on usage patterns and local electricity rates.
Can this system work in homes with existing ductwork? +
No. This wall-mounted mini-split design bypasses ductwork entirely, delivering conditioned air directly to individual rooms through wall-mounted heads. It replaces rather than supplements traditional forced-air systems, making it ideal for retrofits where duct installation is impractical.
How does the 9.4 HSPF2 heating rating compare to gas furnaces? +
HSPF2 measures heat pump efficiency; 9.4 means 94 percent of input energy converts to heat output. In climates above 35°F most winter days, this outperforms gas efficiency on operating costs. Below 5°F, performance degrades, requiring backup heat or supplemental systems.
Is this system better than single-zone alternatives? +
Quad zones excel when rooms need independent temperature control, eliminating thermostat compromises. Single-zone systems cost less upfront but force uniform temperatures across spaces. Choose quad zones for multi-story homes, offices, or guest rooms with different occupancy patterns.
What does the 12-year warranty actually cover? +
Coverage includes compressor defects and component failures when registered online. Labor, refrigerant recharges, and damage from improper installation or maintenance fall outside protection. Always register immediately after professional installation to activate coverage.
