How IndustrialRank Scores Products

The IndustrialRank Score is a calculated metric derived entirely from verified, publicly available product specifications. It is not a user review score, an editorial opinion score, or a paid placement score.

Important: IndustrialRank Scores are calculated metrics based on manufacturer-published and AHRI-certified specifications. They reflect objective performance characteristics — not subjective quality assessments. Always verify specifications on the merchant product page before purchasing.

What the IndustrialRank Score Is (and Isn't)

It is: A weighted composite of four objective, verifiable product attributes derived from certified spec data. Each sub-score maps to a specific data source that you can verify independently.

It is not: A user review score, an editorial recommendation, a paid ranking, or a prediction of long-term reliability. IndustrialRank does not conduct independent laboratory testing.

The Four Sub-Scores

Sub-Score Weight What It Measures Data Source
Efficiency 25% SEER2 cooling efficiency rating (higher = better) AHRI Certified Directory (ahridirectory.org)
Installation 25% Ease of installation: DIY-capable systems score higher Manufacturer product page, EPA 608 requirements
Warranty 25% Combined compressor + parts warranty years Manufacturer warranty documentation, merchant listing
Cold Climate 25% Minimum operating temperature (lower = better) AHRI, NEEP Cold Climate ASHP Listing (neep.org)

Scoring Formula

IndustrialRank Score = (Efficiency + Installation + Warranty + Cold Climate) ÷ 4

Data Sources

  • AHRI Directory — ahridirectory.org — Certified SEER2, HSPF2, and capacity ratings for all HVAC equipment sold in North America.
  • NEEP Cold Climate ASHP Listing — neep.org — Independent third-party cold-climate performance verification.
  • ENERGY STAR — energystar.gov — Certification status and IRA Section 25C tax credit eligibility.
  • Merchant product pages — HVACDirect.com — Warranty terms, pricing, and installation specifications. Verified at time of page creation.
  • Manufacturer documentation — Official product manuals and warranty documents from brand websites.

Update Policy

IndustrialRank scores are recalculated when any of the following change:

  • AHRI-certified SEER2 rating is revised
  • Manufacturer updates warranty terms
  • Product is discontinued or replaced by a new generation
  • New minimum temperature data is published by NEEP

Each product page displays a "Last updated" date in the footer. Prices are not used in score calculations — they change frequently and are shown for informational purposes only.

What We Don't Score

IndustrialRank does not score: build quality (no independent lab testing), noise levels (manufacturer-reported figures are not standardized), aesthetics, color availability, or long-term reliability (insufficient independent data for most brands). These factors are discussed qualitatively in product reviews where relevant.
